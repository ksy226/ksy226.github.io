What is this...? BOOM!! BOOM!!! MISSION STATEMENT!

This website is to provide students or people who are simply interested in further academic pursuit with facilitated access to textbooks and/or future materials that might be interesting to learn about. Generally, this website will help you find your textbooks better with differing price ranged offers by different bookstores and also tries to create crowdsourcing platform for teachers and students or even complete strangers alike to engage in a communicative interactions by means of sharing thought about a text and providing a recommendations for books to study when studying particular academic field or pursuing specific intellectual interests.
